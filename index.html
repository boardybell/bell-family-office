Bond Bell & Co LLC  ·  Fort Wayne, Indiana

Bell Family Office is a service of Bond Bell & Co LLC, offered by invitation to a small number of families. The practice is deliberately small. Bond Bell, who founded the firm, works directly with each family-office client. The work includes the drafting of trust instruments, the coordination of counsel, the management of investment portfolios, and the quiet business of being present through the meetings and decisions that shape how capital passes from one generation to the next.
